Check if your business needs to report

Large companies and large limited liability partnerships (LLPs) may have to report on their payment terms and practices.

You will be asked some questions about your business to find out:

  • if your business needs to report
  • when your next report is due and what dates it should cover
  • what information you need to gather before you file your report

Your answers must be for your business on its own, even if it's part of a group. Every business within the group will need to do this individually.
