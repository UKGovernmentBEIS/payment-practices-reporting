Calculating the number of days taken to pay suppliers
When you work out how many days you take to pay suppliers, day 0 is the day you receive their invoice or timesheet, whether by post, email or otherwise.
Day 1 for calculation purposes is the day after receipt - whatever day of the week that is, including weekends and bank holidays.
The end of the period is when payment is received by the supplier. A payment is viewed as ‘received’ when it’s available to the supplier - for example, when it clears in their bank account.
You don’t need proof that your payment has cleared, but you need to take into account the standard time it takes for payments to clear using your chosen payment method.
