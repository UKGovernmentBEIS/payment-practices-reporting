If you want a colleague who has a Companies House authentication code to publish the report, follow these steps.

  1. Give them the report data, approved by a company director, or a designated member if the business is a limited liability partnership.
  2. Make sure you also tell them what the deadline is to publish the report.
  3. They will need to use their Companies House authentication code to publish the report.
